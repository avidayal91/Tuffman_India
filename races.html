COVID-19 UPDATE

In light of the COVID-19 (Coronavirus outbreak), which was recently declared as a pandemic by the WHO, the situation relating to mass gatherings anywhere may be highly contagious, as advised by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare (MoHFW) and the city administrations.

As a result, we have halted race registrations, for each of the race.

We know that you can’t wait to register for the upcoming races, as an unexpected pause has come in your Tuffman journey.

Be rest assured, as we are monitoring the situation closely, and we will keep updating you on the latest developments on the pandemic from our side, if there's a need for re-scheduling or cancelling the race.

Your's & our's health, well - being & as safety is the priority for us.

Any future communication will be sent, as usual, through e-mails, SMS’s, event website, Facebook events for the races and our social media handles. We advise you to stay updated on the events via the same media(s).

REGARDING CANCELLATIONS – We request you to please refer to event policies present under each individual race's page.

We strictly advise all, to keep safe and stay at home and follow standard hygiene practices to fight against the pandemic swiftly & responsibly.

Report to your nearest health department at the earliest, if you have any symptoms for common cold, fever or have any foreign travel / contact history with infectious people.

KEEP TRAINING AT HOME !!
