RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Tuffman Beach Ultra

It will be a very sexy and mind relaxing ultra experience for all the runners. While running on the Colva Beach, Fishermen’s motor vessels / boats can be seen anchored in a line offshore. The shore is full of Coconut Tress, Tiny Conch and Shells.

Tourists—Indian and foreigners, as well as locals—can be seen in colorful dresses, coming either for a walk or ‘for a change of air’. Many tourists can be seen having a sunbath on the golden sands.

This ultra way will keep you ever fresh and enthusiastic as the race course is full of cool breeze, cool shacks and will provide you a reviving and zealous escapade all together.

Start and Finish Point is at Baywatch Beach (Yoga Centre) for all the race categories.

Facilities & Briefing

RACE BRIEFING

The race course is 5 kms of granular wet beach sand (toughness is equivalent to cemented road) absolutely traffic free which will be perfectly cherished by you.

We’ll talk more about the Race Course, Rules and Regulations, Conditions and a Q/A session would be there followed by heavy carb loading.

AID STATIONS

2 Aid Stations along with 1 Medical Station will be put up in the race as they shall be enough to keep you well hydrated and energy full all the way to and fro in the wet and damp air.

BAG DROP

Convenience Bags can be handed over to Team Tuffman at the end of race briefing with Runner's name tag attached on it which will be supplied in the Goodie bag.

PASTA PARTY & MORE

We are also organizing a Pasta Party for all the runners on 16th November, 2018 i.e, Friday at 17:00 hrs at venue (Camilson's Resort), where we’ll talk about the race, routes and conditions.

Every runner is entitled to have a Personalized T-shirt, Tuffman Cap/Visor & Gym/Hydration Bag, Pre Run Carb loading, Post Race Booze and Meal after race, your excellent photos and videos throughout the race and promised good memories.
