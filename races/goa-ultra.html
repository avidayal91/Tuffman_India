RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Tuffman Beach Ultra

Tuffman proudly announces Tuffman Beach Ultra & Half Marathon Goa - India's first beach ultra's 4th edition at the Sernabatim Beach, Colva (Salcete), Goa.

It will be completely a different running experience for all the runners as it is a beach run. While running on the Colva Beach, people chilling, boats can be seen anchored in a line offshore. The shore is full of coconut trees, tiny conches & shells.

Tourists — Indian and foreigners, as well as locals — can be seen in colorful dresses, coming either for a walk or ‘for a change of air’. Many tourists can be seen having a sunbath on the beach sand.

RACE COURSE & AID STATIONS

The race route is totally flat and will keep you ever fresh and enthusiastic as the race course is full of cool breeze, cool shacks and will provide you a reviving and zealous escapade all together.

The race loop is 5 kms (2.5 kms out & 2.5 kms back) of granular wet beach sand (run feel is equivalent to tar road, gives you a little push from the ground) absolutely traffic free which will be perfectly cherished by you.

The race loop which was earlier 10 kms (5 kms out & 5 kms back) has been shortened due to the rise of water level in the Arabian Sea and multiple puddles / natural drains on the way to Varca Beach.

Enough energy stations will be there enroute at 0 kms & 2.5 kms as they shall be enough to keep you well hydrated and energyful all the way to and fro in the wet and damp air. Full Medical support will be there at start / finish of the race.

We would simply suggest that staying at any of the hotels near Sernabatim Beach would be convenient & comfy for the outstation participants. Simply search Google, with "Hotels near Sernabatim Beach Goa" and it shall show numerous results.
