RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Tuffman Beach Ultra

Tuffman proudly announces Tuffman Beach Ultra & Half Marathon Goa - India's first beach ultra's 4th edition at the Sernabatim Beach, Colva (Salcete), Goa.

It will be a very sexy and mind relaxing running experience for all the runners as it is totally a beach run. While running on the Colva Beach, fishermen’s motor vessels / boats can be seen anchored in a line offshore. The shore is full of coconut trees, tiny conches & shells.

Tourists — Indian and foreigners, as well as locals — can be seen in colorful dresses, coming either for a walk or ‘for a change of air’. Many tourists can be seen having a sunbath on the beach sand.

RACE COURSE & AID STATIONS

The race route is totally flat (no elevation is there in the race) and will keep you ever fresh and enthusiastic as the race course is full of cool breeze, cool shacks and will provide you a reviving and zealous escapade all together.

The race loop is 10 kms of granular wet beach sand (run feel is equivalent to tar road, gives you a little push from the ground) absolutely traffic free which will be perfectly cherished by you.

3 Aid Stations along with 1 Medical Station will be put up in the race as they shall be enough to keep you well hydrated and energyful all the way to and fro in the wet and damp air.
