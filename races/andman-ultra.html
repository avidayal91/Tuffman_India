RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Tuffman Island Ultra

It will be a feeling that you are running somewhere out of India (in abroad) but it’s really a proud thing that you are running in your own country on an island where roads are just touching the sea contours.

You will find Netaji Stadium, Andaman Club, Corbyn Cove Beach, lush green landscapes with coconut trees.

Tourists—Indian and foreigners, as well as locals—can be seen in colorful dresses, coming either for a walk or ‘for a change of air’. Many tourists can be seen having a sunbath on the Corbyn Sands.

It will be a very mind relaxing ultra experience for all the runners. This ultra route will keep you ever fresh and enthusiastic as the race course is full of cool breeze, awesome city surrounds and will provide you a reviving and zealous escape all together.

Start and Finish Point is at Corbyn's Cove Beach for all the race categories.

Facilities & Briefing

RACE BRIEFING

The race course is loop of 10 Kms which is 90% Tar Road, 10% Unpaved Road almost traffic free which will be perfectly cherished by you.

We are also organizing a Race Bib Collection on 16th February, 2019 i.e, Saturday at 17:00 hrs at Wave Restaurant, Corbyn Beach.

AID STATIONS

2 Aid Stations along with 1 Medical Station will be put up in the race as they shall be enough to keep you well hydrated and energyful all the way to and fro in the wet and damp air.

For Half Marathon 5 nos. of stations will be there.

RACE BRIEFING & MORE

We are also organizing a Race Bib Collection on 16th February, 2019 i.e, Saturday at 17:00 hrs at Wave Restaurant, Corbyn Beach.

WHAT'S THERE IN THE COST

Runners who are registered for distances over 30kms and above are entitled to have a Personalized Running DRI-FIT T-shirt and a Visor as the race will be in the Coastal region helping your body to keep fresh during the run.

Runners who are registered for distances like 10kms and 21kms are entitled will have a Non Personalised T Shirt.

OTHER ITEMS IN THE COST

Bib with Running chip, Medal, Certificate, Hydration and Nutrition during the race and your photos throughout and video clips at finish line.

Perfect sunrise and sunset, picturesque and a sea dip at the beach and much more.

Accomodations & Instructions

ACCOMMODATION
