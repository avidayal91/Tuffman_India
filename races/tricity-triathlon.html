About Tricity Triathlon

"The City Beautiful" Chandigarh is a city and a union territory in India that serves as the capital of the two neighbouring states of Haryana and Punjab. The metropolitan area of Chandigarh–Mohali–Panchkula collectively forms a Tri-City.

SWIM

Tuffman Tricity Triathlon will begin with a 1.5 KM swim in the 50 mts. pool. We have 8 lines in the pool and in each line 2 swimmers will swim.

BIKE

After swim, the athletes will have a transition to the bike leg with 80% flat course crossing through a town Mullanpur, 15 KM upto The Oberoi Sukhvilas Gate and then turning back to take a loop of 12.5Km to reach finish line that is The Oberoi Sukhvilas 40km (2 loops) in total.

RUN

The 10 KM run course is 5 KM long, athletes will start the run out of transition from the Bike Leg heading through entrance gate of The Oberoi Sukhvilas towards New Chandigarh.
