About Tuffman Snow Ultra

It will be a spine chilling and mind numbing running experience for all the runners while running on this snow ultra.

Enroute you all will experience Jana Falls while running to Jana Village Top where you can have a sumptuous food reaching Jana Falls which will be the U Turn. On finishing your race, reaching the camp, you will enjoy some local folk tunes and dance of Himachal.

This ultra way will keep you ever chilled.

Facilities & Briefing

RACE BRIEFING

Start and Finish Point is Camp Exotica Manali for all the race categories.

The race course is a combination of 17 kms tar road and 1.5K Trail which shall be covered with snow on the roadsides hopefully and snow capped mountains all around (absolutely traffic free) which shall be perfectly lucrative to you. However, somewhere patches of road are not fine. So we can consider the route to be 5% trail and 95% tar road with good elevation.

AID STATIONS

5 Aid Stations along with 1 Medical Station will be put up in the race as they shall be enough to keep you well hydrated and energyful all the way to and fro in the dry snowy weather.

PASTA PARTY & MORE

We are also organizing a Pasta Party for all the runners on 22nd March, 2019 i.e, Friday at 16:45 hrs at venue, where we’ll talk about the race, routes and conditions for all race categories.

WHAT'S THERE IN THE COST

Every runner (Except 10K & 21K Runner without Tuffman Goodies & Armed Forces Ticket) is entitled to have a Good Warm Sweatshirt, a T Shirt and Ultra Cap as the race is in March and Manali it is.

OTHER ITEMS IN THE COST

Bib with Timing Chip, Medal, E- Certificate, Hydration and Nutrition during the race, Pre Run Pasta party, Post Race Massage, Booze with local Himachali Tunes and Dance, and your photos throughout and video clips at finish line.

Perfect snowy and chilly weather in Manali and waterfall at U Turn :D
