About Tuffman Hussainiwala Border Half Marathon

Tuffman announces Tuffman Hussainiwala Border Half Marathon Ferozepur as a posthumous run for the shaheed legends Bhagat Singh, Shivaram Rajguru & Sukhdev Thapar.

It's time, we move beyond paying mere lip-service to their sacrifice once every year & adopt the examples they set – that of deep love for one’s nation, dedication to their cause and an unquenchable thirst for knowledge – in our own lives. A run for these martyr's, where they were martyred, perhaps, the most meaningful tribute we can pay to these heroes.

Facilities & Briefing

RACE BRIEFING

Race will start and finish at The Iconic Saragarhi Memorial Gurudwara, the place which commemorates 21 Sikh soldiers of the 36th Sikh Regiment. The soldiers died while protecting the Fort Saragarhi in Waziristan (in Afghanistan) where the battle of Saragarhi was fought as showcased in the movie - Kesari.

Full race course is on an excellent paved road which leads to the iconic Hussainiwala Border touching Pakistan. Runners will be crossing the Sutlej river twice while running on the race route.

One can also achieve personal best timings as the route is near to flat (50 metres elevation gain) & break one's own past records as one trains well.

AID STATIONS

There will be enough aid stations along with the medical support to keep one ever hydrated.
