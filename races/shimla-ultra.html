Facilities & Briefing

RACE BRIEFING

On 7th June, 2019 i.e, Friday at 14:00 hrs – 17:00 hrs at Camp Mashobra Greens (Our Hospitality Partner) participants can collect their bib along with the goodie bags there at the race expo and 17:00 hrs onwards we’ll talk about the race course, rules and regulations, conditions and a Q/A session which would be followed by carb loading.

80 K – 4 ITRA Points – Loop of 40K Elevation Gain 6000 ft.
50 K – 2 ITRA Points – Loop of 40K and U Turn 5K Elevation Gain 3000 ft.
30K – Going till 15K and turn back on the race route - Elevation Gain 2200-2500 ft.
21K – Going till 10.5K and turn back on the race route - Elevation Gain 1500 ft.
10K – Going till 5K and turn back on the race route.

Minimum Age: All athletes must be 18 or older on race day. (10 years to be minimum for 5K)

AID STATIONS

Aid Stations will be at every 5K.

BAG DROP (Only for 80K & 50K)

Convenience Bags can be handed over to Team Tuffman at the end of race briefing with Runner's name tag attached on it which will be supplied in the Goodie bag.

INCLUSIONS FOR 80K / 50K

Every runner is entitled to have an Excellent Quality Personalised Tuffman T-Shirt, Premium Goodie Bag, Running Bib Belt, Pre run carb loading, post race booze, hot meal after race, your excellent photos, videos throughout the race, promised good memories, medal and digital race certificate post completion of the race.

INCLUSIONS FOR 30K / 21K / 10K / 5K

Every runner is entitled to have an Excellent Quality Tuffman T-Shirt, Tuffman Visor & Goodie Bag, Running Bib Belt, Pre run carb loading, post race booze, hot meal after race, your excellent photos, videos throughout the race, promised good memories, medal and digital race certificate post completion of the race.

Accomodations & Instructions

ACCOMMODATION

For your convenience, we would simply suggest that staying at the camp will be more comfy as the race starts from Camp Mashobra Greens only & is well equipped for a hospitable stay for your family and friends.

Accommodation includes all meals i.e Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner.

Superb bamboo cottages with attached washroom, Canvas tents with common washrooms are there for your comfortable stay. Nice jungle walks, obstacles around base camp, adventure activities offered by camp for your family and friends.

Delicious food amidst of jungle, soothing tunes and bonfire ... all will be there at Camp Mashobra Greens

CANCELLATION POLICY

Any requests received before 31st March, 2019 will be eligible for a 50% refund of event and accommodation entry fee paid.

Any requests received after 31st March, 2019 will not be eligible for a refund / transfer under any circumstances.
