About Shimla Ultra

Tuffman is very happy and proud to announce the 6th Edition of Tuffman Shimla Ultra & Half Marathon Mashobra (Affiliated by Athletics Federation of India)

It will be again a very top-notch and challenging ultra experience for all the runners as its of the most toughest hill ultras in India in claimed by people. This ultra trail will cover all the exquisite views of the dense woods at Mashobra, Shimla and will provide you a rejuvenating and revamping experience all together.

Facilities & Briefing

RACE BRIEFING

On 26th June, 2020 i.e, Friday at 13:30 hrs – 17:00 hrs at Camp Mashobra Greens (Our Hospitality Partner) – Participants can collect their bib along with the goodie bags there at the race expo and 17:00 hrs onwards we’ll talk about the race course, rules and regulations, conditions and a Q/A session which would be followed by carb loading.

Elite 100K – 4 ITRA Points, Loop & Elevation gain will be informed soon.
80K – 4 ITRA Points – 4 ITRA Points – Loop of 40K with Elevation Gain - 4950 mtrs.
50K – 3 ITRA Points – Loop of 40K and U Turn 5K with Elevation Gain - 2960 mtrs.
30K – 2 ITRA Points - Going till 15K and turn back on the same route with Elevation Gain - 2550 mtrs.
21K – 1 ITRA Points - 1 ITRA Point – Going till 10.5K and turn back on the same route with Elevation Gain - 1250 mtrs.
10K – Going till 5K and turn back on the same route with Elevation Gain - 950 mtrs.
Tuffkids 5K - Loop of 2.5K and turn back on the same route with Elevation Gain - 600 mtrs.

Minimum Age: All athletes must be 18 or older on race day.
(10 years to be minimum for 5K)

AID STATIONS

Aid Stations will be at every 5K.

BAG DROP (Only for 100K, 80K & 50K)

Convenience Bags can be handed over to our team at the end of race briefing with runner's name tag attached on it which will be supplied in the goodie bag.

INCLUSIONS FOR 100K / 80K / 50K

Every runner is entitled to have an Excellent Quality Personalised Tuffman T–Shirt, Premium Goodie Bag, Pre Run Carb Loading, Post Race Booze, Hot Meal After Race, Your Excellent Photos & Videos throughout the race, Promised Good Memories, Tuffman Finisher's Medal, Digital Race Certificate post completion of the race & Free BIB WISE photos delivery.

INCLUSIONS FOR 30K / 21K / 10K / 5K

Every runner is entitled to have an Excellent Quality Tuffman T–Shirt, Goodie Bag, Pre Run Carb Loading, Post Race Booze (Not to 5K Participants), Hot Meal After Race, Your Excellent Photos & Videos throughout the race, Promised Good Memories, Tuffman Finisher's Medal, Digital Race Certificate post completion of the race & Free BIB WISE photos delivery.

Accomodations & Instructions

ACCOMMODATION

For your convenience, we would simply suggest that staying at the camp will be more comfy as the race starts from Camp Mashobra Greens only & is well equipped for a hospitable stay for your family and friends.

Accommodation includes all meals i.e Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner.

Superb bamboo cottages with attached washroom, Canvas tents with common washrooms are there for your comfortable stay. Nice jungle walks, obstacles around base camp, adventure activities offered by camp for your family and friends.

Delicious food amidst of jungle, soothing tunes and bonfire ... all will be there at Camp Mashobra Greens

Accommodation at the best possible rates is available at Camp Mashobra Greens as per the above stated options which are provided to everyone for 2 days on suitable sharing basis (TWIN / TRIPLE / QUAD) which includes all meals for 2 days i.e Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner, if purchased through Townscript.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Any cancellation / refund / withdrawl requests received before 31st March, 2020 will be eligible for a 50% refund of event & accommodation entry fee paid (excluding processing fee).

Any cancellation / refund / withdrawl requests received after 31st March, 2020 will not be eligible for a refund of fee / transfer of ticket under any circumstances.

In case of occurrence of any unforeseeable circumstances / events / situations due to any x-factor leading upto event cancellation, the event fees paid wouldn't be refunded under any circumstances.
