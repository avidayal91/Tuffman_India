RACE COURSE & AID STATIONS

Race will start and finish at Army Ground situated in middle of Kasauli Hills. Full course is on Tar through mist, scenic hills beauty, local villages. There will be enough aid stations along with the medical support to keep you ever hydrated.

Highest point of the race - 1882 metres
Lowest point of the race - 1200 metres

RACE INCLUSIONS

21K & 10K Runners are entitled to have an Excellent Quality DRI - FIT T-Shirt, Goodie Bag, Timing Bib, Beautiful Tuffman Finisher's Medal, Finisher Digital Certificate, Pre - During - Post Race Hydration, Medical Support, Post Race Nutritious Refreshments, Bib wise photos for every category runner.

5K & 3K Runners are entitled to have a Quality DRI FIT T-Shirt, Goodie Bag, Non Timed Bib, Beautiful Tuffman Finisher's Medal, Pre - During - Post Race Hydration, Medical Support, Post Race Nutritious Refreshments, Bib wise photos for every category runner.

MINIMUM AGE FOR PARTICIPATION

  • All athletes must be 18 years or older on the race day for Timed Half Marathon 21 kms running.
  • All athletes must be 14 years or older on the race day for Timed Internationally acclaimed 10 kms running.
  • All athletes must be 8 years or older on the race day for Non Timed Fun Run 5 & 3 kms running.

SUGGESTED ACCOMODATION

WelcomHeritage Glenview Resorts, Kasauli is the five star hotel by WelcomHeritage Hotels which is further led by ITC Hotels. Book in advance at the earliest to secure your accommodation as weekends are always busy for hotels in the region.

PARKING FOR THE RACE DAY & BIB EXPO

Around 300 parking slots will be available on 16th & 17th November, 2019 at the following places.

  • Parking Area, St. Mary’s Convent School, Kasauli
  • Parking Area, Military Hospital (MH), Kasauli
  • Public Parking Area, Near Major Dhyanchand Statue

NOTE: Parking anywhere will be completely at owner’s / driver’s risk and the organizer will completely be not responsible for any theft / loss of any kind of article.
