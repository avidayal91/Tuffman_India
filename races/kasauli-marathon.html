RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Kasauli Hills Half Marathon

Race will start and finish at Army Ground situated in middle of Kasauli Hills. Full course is on Tar through mist, scenic hills beauty, local villages.

Highest Point Of Race 1882m.
Lowest Point Of Race 1200m.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Race will start and finish from Army Ground Kasauli, situated in middle of Kasauli Hills, near Bus Stand, Kasauli.

RACE INCLUSIONS

Every runner is entitled to have an Excellent Quality DRI FIT T-Shirt (21K & 10K Participants), Quality DRI FIT T-Shirt (5K & 3K Participants), Finisher Medal, Bib (Timed for 21K & 10K & Non Timed for 5K & 3K), Post Race Refreshment (Including 5K & 3K Participants), E - Certificate, Bib Wise Photos, Hydration & Medical support during the run and lots of promised fun.

SUGGESTED ACCOMODATION

Hospitality Partner - WelcomHeritage Glenview Resorts, Kasauli is the five star hotel by WelcomHeritage Hotels which is further led by ITC Hotels.
