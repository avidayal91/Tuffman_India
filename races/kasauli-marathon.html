RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Kasauli Hills Half Marathon

Race will start and finish at Army Ground situated in middle of Kasauli Hills. Full course is on Tar through mist, scenic hills beauty, local villages.

Highest Point Of Race 1882m.
Lowest Point Of Race 1200m.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Race will start and finish from Army Ground Kasauli, situated in middle of Kasauli Hills, near Bus Stand, Kasauli.

ATTENTION!: Roads to the venue will get closed at 6:00 AM, its a request you reach at the venue before 6:00 else after route closure, you have to park your vehicle 2kms away from the venue and then have to walk all the upto there.
