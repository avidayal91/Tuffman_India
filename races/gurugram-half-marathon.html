RACE BRIEFING

Race will start and finish at the Surface Parking Space between DLF Gateway Tower (DLF Ship Building) & We Work Building – Opposite to CyberHub, Gurugram. Full race course is on an excellent paved road on which you can achieve you personal best timings and break their own records as they train well.

AID STATIONS

There will be enough aid stations along with the medical support to keep you ever hydrated.

Tuffman Impact League

Tuffman welcomes Impact App as our Philanthropy Partner as we raise INR 10 for a charity of your choice with every km you cover on Impact.

Simply pick a social cause, walk/jog and do good for society while getting fit.

Tuffman Impact App League (1st April - 19th April)
A 19-day pre-marathon event where you can support a social cause with your daily practice run.

STEPS TO JOIN THE LEAGUE

1. Download Impact App from play store/app store onelink.to/impact

2. Enter the code 'TUFFGGN' in the league section.

3. Choose a cause to support and Let's Go.

Register before 31st March, 2019 to get yourself enrolled in the league.

Top 3 contributors and the most consistent person in the Tuffman Impact App League to win special prizes on the Marathon day i.e. 21st April.
