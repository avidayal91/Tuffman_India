Facilities & Briefing

AGE & GENDER CATEGORIES

The results & podium finishers, based on chip timing for all will be declared according to the Age & Gender Wise Categories for the distances as mentioned below.

Age & Gender Categories in 21 Km & 10 Km are :-

  • 18 years to 30.364 years of age
  • 31 years to 40.364 years of age
  • 41 years to 50.364 years of age
  • 51 years of age and above

Age & Gender Categories in TuffKids 5K are :-

  • 8 years to 11.364 years
  • 12 years to 16 years

Please note that there would be no Age or Gender Categories in the Non-Timed 5 Km & 3 Km races.

RACE BRIEFING

Race will start and finish at the Surface Parking Space between DLF Gateway Tower (DLF Ship Building) & We Work Building – Opposite to CyberHub, Gurugram.

Full race course is on an excellent paved road on which runners can achieve your personal best timings & break your own past records as you train well. There will be enough aid stations along with the medical support to keep you ever hydrated.

AID STATIONS

There will be enough aid stations along with the medical support to keep you ever hydrated.

INCLUSIONS FOR PARTICIPANTS

21K & 10K Runners are entitled to have an Excellent Quality DRI-FIT T-Shirt, Goodie Bag, Timing Bib, Beautiful Tuffman Finisher's Medal, Finisher Digital Certificate, Pre - During - Post Race Hydration, Medical Support, Post Race Nutritious Refreshments, Bib wise photos for every category runner.

5K & 3K Runners are entitled to have a Quality DRI-FIT T-Shirt, Timing Bib (Only for TuffKids), Non-Timed Bib for Non Timed 5K & 3K, Beautiful Finisher's Medal, Finisher Digital Certificate for Timed Racers, Pre - During - Post Race Hydration, Medical Support, Post Race Nutritious Refreshments, Bib wise photos for every category runner.
