About Tricity Stadium Run

Tuffman invites you to run to run & test your tough at one of the most awaited stadium runs of the year.

Tuffman 24 Hrs Stadium Run will test your endurance and grit that resides inside everyone.

Stadium run is a platform where you can test your physical as well as mental capabilities. It will be the 2nd edition of the stadium run in Chandigarh which is affliated by the Athletics Federation of India (AFI).

The stadium is famous for its well - maintained track and many athletes use this facility to train to their maximum potential and you will experience a running festival at the same place.

Important Instructions

SLOT PREFERNECES

Slot Preference is kept open to all and is totally subject to availability till the vacant spot(s) lasts. Once slots are chosen, no changes would be entertained for the same.

PARTICIPATION CONFIRMATION

Subject to Race Director's Approval and qualification of eligibility criteria.

TITLE CRITERIA(S)

  • 161K and above distance finisher in 24H category will be an Ultra Tuffman.
  • 100K finisher in 100K distance category under 15 hours will be a Tuffman.
  • 80K and above distance finisher in 12H category will be a Tuffman.
  • 50K and above distance finisher in 6H category will be a Half Tuffman.
  • Rest all finishers under 3H or Team Relay category will be Inspiring Tuffman.

FINISHING CRITERIA(S)

Please find the cutoff criteria(s) in the mentioned categories in respect to the MINIMUM DISTANCES & TIME BOTH that has to be covered in the stadium run.

Category - Distance - Minimum Time

  • For 3 hrs - 18 Kms (No Time Limit)
  • For 6 hrs - 35 Kms (No Time Limit)
  • For 12 hrs - 60 Kms - 10 hrs
  • For 24 hrs - 120 Kms - 20 hrs
  • For Team Relay - 40 Kms - 1 hr each member (Total 40 Kms by three team members in 6 hrs & 1 hr atleast BOTH to be covered by each team member on the track)
