About Tricity Stadium Run

Tuffman invites you to run where the athletes train - Stadium. It will also be tricity's first ever Stadium Run in Chandigarh.

Tuffman 24 Hrs Stadium Run will test your endurance and grit that resides inside everyone. This is a place where runners can test their physical as well as mental capabilities.

The stadium is famous for its well - maintained track and many athletes use this facility to train to their maximum potential and you will experience a running festival at the same place.

Special Discount available for all Indian Armed Forces personnels and their dependants.
