RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Tricity Stadium Run

Race will be conducted in Tau Devi Lal Stadium, Panchkula during a pleasant weather of Chandigarh Region.

This stadium is famous for its well maintained track and many athletes use this facility to train to their maximum potential.

This is going to be first ever stadium run in Tricity region where runners can test their physical as well as mental capabilities.
