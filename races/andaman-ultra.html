Be rest assured, as we are monitoring the situation closely, and we will keep updating you on the latest developments on the pandemic from our side, if there's a need for re-scheduling or cancelling the race.

Your's & our's health, well - being & as safety is the priority for us.

Any future communication will be sent, as usual, through e-mails, SMS’s, event website, Facebook events for the races and our social media handles. We advise you to stay updated on the events via the same media(s).

REGARDING CANCELLATIONS – We request you to please refer to event policies present under each individual race's page.

We strictly advise all, to keep safe and stay at home and follow standard hygiene practices to fight against the pandemic swiftly & responsibly.

Report to your nearest health department at the earliest, if you have any symptoms for common cold, fever or have any foreign travel / contact history with infectious people.

RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Tuffman Island Ultra

It will be a feeling that you are running somewhere out of India (in abroad) and it’s really a proud thing that you are running in your own country on an island where the roads are just touching the sea contours.

You will find Netaji Stadium, Andaman Club, Corbyn Cove Beach, lush green landscapes with coconut trees.

Tourists—Indian and foreigners, as well as locals—can be seen in colorful dresses, coming either for a walk or ‘for a change of air’. Many tourists can be seen having a sunbath on the Corbyn Sands.

A promised mind - relaxing ultra experience for all the runners. This ultra route will keep you ever fresh and enthusiastic as the race course is full of cool breeze, longer distances starts in the wee hours of the morning, awesome city surrounds and will provide you a reviving and zealous escape all together, with your partner.

Start and Finish Point is at Corbyn's Cove Beach for all the race categories.

Facilities & Briefing

RACE COURSE

Race course is a loop of 10 Kms (5 kms out and 5 kms back) which is of 90% Tar Road & 10% Unpaved Road almost traffic free which will be perfectly cherished by you with very gradual inclines & declines.

AID STATIONS

3 Energy Stations & 2 Hydration Stations (each at a gap of 2 or 2.5 kms) along with 1 Medical Station (at the start) will be put up in the race as they shall be enough to keep you well Hydrated & Energetic and energyful all the way to and fro in the wet and damp air.

For Half Marathon 5 nos. of stations will be there.

Accomodations & Instructions
