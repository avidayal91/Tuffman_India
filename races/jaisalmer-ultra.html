RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Tuffman Desert Ultra

Tuffman proudly announces 4th edition of India's most testing sand pile ultra run in The Great Indian Thar Desert.

READ AGAIN - It's not running on the road and it's a run on the sand pile, in the desert.

It will be a silky and carpet feeling running experience for all the runners while running on the sand pile region.

You will experience running on the sand pile for almost entire route where temperatures usually range from bone chilling cold at night to burning hot sunny in day.

Start and Finish Point is Mirvana Nature Resort for all the race categories.

Facilities & Briefing

RACE COURSE

RACE ROUTE For 220 Kms / 161 Kms (100 Miles) / 24 Hours Participants:
The race loop length is 1 km unpaved gravel & sand pile (carpet feeling) absolutely traffic free which will be perfectly cherished by you.

RACE ROUTE For 80 Kms / 50 Kms / 32 Kms / 21 Kms / 10 Kms Participants:
The race loop length is 16 kms sand pile (carpet feeling) absolutely traffic free which will be perfectly cherished by you. Nilgai and Deers can be spotted easily beside the race route.

AID STATIONS

3 Aid Stations along with 1 Medical Station will be put up in the race as they shall be enough to keep you well hydrated and energyful all the way to and fro in the dry air.

RACE FEE INCLUSIONS

**220K / 161K (100 Miles) / 24 Hours / 80K / 50K Runners are entitled to have an Excellent Quality Personalised DRI - FIT T-Shirt, Light Winter Fleece, Premium Goodie Bag, Timing Bib, Beautiful Tuffman Finisher's Medal, Ultra Cap, Finisher Digital Certificate, Pre - During - Post Race Hydration, Medical Support, Post Race Nutritious Meal, Post Race Booze with Local Rajasthani Tunes & Dance at Finish, Promised Good Memories.

32K / 21K / 10K Runners are entitled to have an Excellent Quality DRI - FIT T-Shirt, Goodie Bag, Timing Bib, Beautiful Tuffman Finisher's Medal, Finisher Digital Certificate, Pre - During - Post Race Hydration, Medical Support, Post Race Nutritious Meal, Local Rajasthani Tunes & Dance at Finish, Promised Good Memories.

NOTE: The 161K (100 Miles) & 220K runners will also get a qualifying medal for attending the Ultra Tuffman category in the race briefing.
