RACE INTRO & BRIEF

About Tuffman Desert Ultra

It will be a silky and carpet feeling running experience for all the runners while running on the sand pile region. Nilgai and Deer can be spotted easily beside the race route.

You all will experience running on Sand Dunes for some 50mt or 100mt in the middle of route and a Big Dune of 200mt on U Turn. Here on U Turn you will enjoy some local folk tunes and dance of Rajasthan.

This ultra way will keep you ever awesome and enthusiastic as the race course is full of cool breeze and sand.

Start and Finish Point is Mirvana Nature Resort for all the race categories.

Facilities & Briefing

RACE BRIEFING

The race loop is 16 kms sand pile (carpet feeling) absolutely traffic free which will be perfectly cherished by you.

We’ll talk more about the Race Course, Rules and Regulations, Conditions and a Q/A session would be there followed by heavy carb loading.

AID STATIONS

3 Aid Stations along with 1 Medical Station will be put up in the race as they shall be enough to keep you well hydrated and energyful all the way to and fro in the dry air.

GOODIES

Every runner is entitled to have a Personalised T-shirt and a good light winter fleece as the race will be in the sand pile region which will be remain chilly for some early morning and late night hours.

NOTE:- The 100 Mile Runner will get a Qualifying Medal for attending the Ultra Tuffman 100 Miler in the race briefing.

Goodie Bag, Bib with Running chip, Medal, Certificate, Hydration and Nutrition during the race, Pre Run Pasta, Post Race Booze with local Rajasthani Tunes and Dance, and your photos throughout and video clips at finish line.

PASTA PARTY & MORE

We are also organizing Pasta ceremony for all the runners on 21 December, 2018 i.e., Friday at 17:00 hrs at venue, where we’ll talk about the race, routes and conditions for runners who are running 20K / 30K / 50K / 80K race categories.

And the same will be on 20 December, 2018 i.e., Thursday for 24 Hrs & 100 Mile category runners.

A Masseur at Start Point which will be available during the race round the clock.

Accomodations & Instructions

ACCOMMODATION

Accommodation and food is organized at Mirvana Nature Resort (Our Hospitality Partner - A Four Star Facility) for runners and his/her mates.

Accommodation is there for your comfy stay and is made readily available for you at minimum rates. Beautiful Swimming Pool, Paid Spa will be there in resort. For your sight scenes, you can directly contact us or the manager at reception.

Authentic Rajasthani food, heavy carb - loading and lots of booze, soothing folk tunes and dances. These all will be there at the Mirvana Nature Resort.
