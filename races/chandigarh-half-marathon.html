Facilities & Briefing

RACE COURSE & AID STATIONS

Race will start and finish at the Open Hand Monument, Near to Rock Garden, Chandigarh. Full race course is on an excellent paved road on which runners can achieve their personal best timings and break their own records as they train well. There will be enough aid stations along with the medical support to keep them ever hydrated.

INCLUSION FOR PARTICIPANTS

Every runner is entitled to have an Excellent Quality Dri-Fit T-shirt (21K & 10K) and Quality DRI FIT T-Shirt (5K & 3K), Timing Bib for 21K & 10K (5K & 3K Fun Run - Non Timed), Beautiful Finisher's Medal, Finisher Digital Certificate for Timed Racers, Pre-During-Post Race Hydration, Post Race Refreshments (Including 5K & 3K Participant), Bib Wise Photographs for every category runner.
